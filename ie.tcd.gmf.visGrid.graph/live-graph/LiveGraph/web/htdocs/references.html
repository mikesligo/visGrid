References - who uses LiveGraph?

Dear users, we need your help!
We would like to know what you are using LiveGraph for.

  • Important for you:
    knowing about you will help other users to decide whether LiveGraph is right for them.
  • Important for us:
    knowing about our users will help us making LiveGraph better for you.
Please, spend 30 seconds of your time and let us know who you are and how you are using LiveGraph.
We are very happy to put links to your projects here. Get in touch via our requests and reports forum.
